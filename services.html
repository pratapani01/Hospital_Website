Comprehensive Healthcare Services & Facilities

Our hospital provides a wide range of clinical and support services — available 24/7 to serve you and your family.

Our Key Departments & Facilities

Round-the-clock emergency care with rapid triage, resuscitation bays, on-call specialists and a fully equipped trauma area for urgent life-saving interventions.

  • Rapid triage and resuscitation
  • Dedicated trauma team
  • Immediate access to imaging & labs

Multi-disciplinary ICU for critically ill patients with ventilatory support, monitoring and specialist intensivist care.

  • Ventilator support and invasive monitoring
  • Step-down unit & high-dependency care
  • 24x7 critical care nursing

General medicine and surgical services covering inpatient and outpatient care, with modern OT setups for elective and emergency surgeries.

  • Fully equipped operating theatres
  • Pre-op and post-op care
  • Ambulatory (daycare) procedures

Experienced physicians for acute and chronic medical conditions with diagnostic support and personalized care plans.

  • Diabetes and hypertension management
  • Infectious disease care
  • Chronic disease follow-up clinics

Comprehensive general surgical services — emergency and elective — supported by modern anesthesia and post-op care.

  • Open and laparoscopic surgery
  • Hernia, appendicitis and GI surgeries
  • Post-operative rehabilitation

Maternal and reproductive health services including antenatal care, normal & assisted deliveries and gynecologic surgery.

  • Antenatal & postnatal care
  • Normal and cesarean deliveries
  • Women’s health clinics and family planning

Child-friendly clinical services for infants, children and adolescents with immunization, growth monitoring and pediatric emergency care.

  • Well-child visits & vaccinations
  • Pediatric emergency & inpatient care
  • Neonatal support & referrals

Advanced laparoscopic procedures for quicker recovery, smaller scars and shorter hospital stays.

  • Gall bladder, appendicectomy and hernia repair
  • Diagnostic laparoscopy
  • Post-op pain management clinic

Bone, joint and trauma management including fracture care, joint replacements and sports injury treatment.

  • Fracture fixation & casting
  • Joint replacement services
  • Arthroscopy and sports medicine

In-house laboratory services supporting timely diagnosis with routine and specialized tests.

  • Biochemistry, hematology, microbiology
  • Pre-op and emergency lab testing
  • Sample collection & home collection (on request)

Complementary homoeopathic consultations for patients seeking alternative medical approaches alongside conventional care.

  • Outpatient consultations
  • Chronic condition support

Emergency ambulance services with trained paramedics and life-support equipment for rapid transport.

  • Basic and advanced life support ambulances
  • Inter-hospital transfers and emergency pickup
  • Telephone dispatch & GPS-enabled routing

Additional services designed to improve patient convenience, outcomes and continuity of care.

  • Pharmacy & Medical Store: On-site pharmacy open extended hours.
  • Diagnostic Imaging: X-ray, Ultrasound, CT scan (arrange locally or via partner).
  • Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation: Post-operative and sports rehab program.
  • Dialysis Support: Tie-ups or in-house dialysis support for chronic kidney patients.
  • Telemedicine / Online Consultations: Remote follow-ups and specialist teleconsults.
  • Preventive Health Checkups: Customizable health packages and executive checkups.
  • Insurance & Cashless Support: Billing & insurance assistance for cashless claims.
  • Nutrition & Counseling: Dietitians, mental health counselors and smoking cessation support.
  • Blood Bank Partnerships: Tie-ups for blood & blood component support.
  • Homecare Services: Post-discharge nursing and sample collection at home (on request).
